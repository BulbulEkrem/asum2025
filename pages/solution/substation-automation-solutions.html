Substation Automation Solutions

Substation Automation System (SAS) provides protection, control, automation, monitoring, and communication capabilities as a part of a comprehensive substation control and monitoring solution.

TEC Global deliver highly reliable, highly flexible turnkey substation automation solutions. Whether upgrading existing substations or delivering complete protection and automation solutions for new greenfield sites, TEC Global provides cost effective solutions based on open standards to simplify future expansion and maintainability and minimise lifetime costs. Our solutions range from complete IEC61850 based systems to non-IEC61850 IEDs and combinations of both, allowing utilities to move to new technologies without discarding current investments.

We provide a full range of services including: control, protection and communications design, system build, installation, grading settings, commissioning and support. Our systems and toolsets support multi-vendor device integration allowing utilities to integrate best in class relays.

Solutions include compact medium voltage systems to large sophisticated systems at high voltage with multi-user workstations.

Whether conventional protection and control schemes or full digital substations, our Substation Automation Solutions feature leading IEDs, protection relays, metering, and monitoring solutions. Based on the latest IEC 61850 standards, our solutions combine improved interoperability, connectivity and security, with an efficient and easy-to-use operator interface.
