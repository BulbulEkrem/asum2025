TEC GLOBAL IS AWARDED AND SIGNED CONTRACT FOR UNDP AL-QAIM SUBSTATION AND AL-QUNAITREA SUBSTATION PROJECTS IN IRAQ.

16/12/2020

TEC Global has been awarded and signed contract for the complete Electrical Works Scope of UNDP (United Nations Development Programme) Al-Qaim Substation and Al-Qunaitrea Substation in Iraq.

By this agreement, TEC Global will be responsible of complete package: design, manufacturing, and supply of following electrical equipment.

Scope of Supply of TEC Global for both Substation:

  • 11kV Medium Voltage, Metal-Clad AIS Switchgear
  • 36kV Medium Voltage, Metal-Clad AIS Switchgear
  • Complete renewal of Protection relays and Panels with protection Engineering of Al-Qaim SS
  • Rehabilitation and retrofit works of Al-Qaim SS
  • Control Panels 
  • LV AC Distribution Board 
  • DC Distribution Boards
  • DC System / Battery & Battery-Charger & UPS
  • Engineering & Design
  • Project Management
